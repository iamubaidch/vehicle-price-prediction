How To Use

Getting Started with Auto Price Predictor

  • How do I choose the correct vehicle make?

    Browse and select from our comprehensive dropdown list of automotive brands.

  • Where do I find the vehicle model selection?

    Once you've selected the make, choose the exact model from the subsequent dropdown menu.

  • How do I specify the transmission type?

    Click on the transmission dropdown and select the type that matches your vehicle - either 'Automatic' or 'Manual'.

  • Where do I input the model year?

    Find and select the year your vehicle was manufactured from our 'Model Year' dropdown menu, ensuring accuracy for a precise valuation.

  • What if I'm not sure about the fuel type?

    Refer to your vehicle's manual or registration documents to confirm if it's 'Petrol', 'Diesel', or 'Hybrid', then choose the appropriate option from our fuel type dropdown.

  • How important is selecting the right color?

    While color can influence resale value, for an accurate prediction, select the color that corresponds exactly to your vehicle's exterior from our color options.

  • Does registration city impact price prediction?

    Yes, select the city where your vehicle is registered for location-specific valuations.

  • What should I input for mileage?

    Enter the current mileage reading from your vehicle's odometer. Accurate mileage is crucial for a precise price estimate.
